Thugs¶

This page offers pre-built thugs, criminals and other shady characters. A thug might be a common thief, street soldier of a criminal gang or just a bouncer at a night club. Their tier might be representative within a hierarchy of an organised crime syndicate, with low level soldiers being rookies or experienced, while capos are expert thugs, and a crime boss might even be a veteran thug.
